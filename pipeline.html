Walk-Forward Optimization

Runs overnight from 8 PM to 8 AM. The optimizer tunes strategy parameters across 6 rolling time windows, testing up to 500 parameter combinations per window. This ensures the strategy works across different market periods, not just one lucky stretch.

06
Stress Test

Tier 2 Evaluation

The optimized strategy is evaluated strictly on data it has never seen before against six metrics: Profit Factor, Sharpe Ratio, Max Drawdown, Trade Count, Expectancy, and Recovery Factor. These thresholds are dynamic - a strategy tested on 5 years of data has different requirements than one tested on 2 years. Only strategies that prove a real edge on unseen data advance.

Unseen Data Only
Dynamic Thresholds
Profit Factor · Sharpe · Drawdown
07
Autonomous

Crash Stress Testing

The strategy is forced into trades during three real historical crashes: COVID (March 2020), Luna collapse (May 2022), and FTX collapse (November 2022). Both long and short entries are tested. Crash performance is factored into the overall evaluation - a strategy that holds up under extreme conditions carries more confidence into the approval step.

Human Gate

Live Promotion

The finish line. When a paper trading strategy builds enough track record and the Performance Monitor ranks it as the top performer for its ticker, it becomes eligible for the one live trading slot. The operator makes the final call - reviewing the strategy's real trading history, market condition performance, and risk metrics before promoting it to live capital. This is the second and final human decision in the entire pipeline.
